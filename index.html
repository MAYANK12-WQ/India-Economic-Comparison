üìä Key Economic Findings at a Glance

GDP Growth

UPA averaged 7.8% growth vs NDA's 6.1% (includes COVID -6.6%). Excluding COVID, NDA averages 7.1%.

Inflation Control

NDA achieved 5.0% average inflation vs UPA's 8.1% - a significant improvement in price stability.

FDI Attraction

NDA attracted $665B cumulative FDI vs UPA's $303B - more than double the foreign investment.

Forex Reserves

Reserves grew from $141B (2004) to $645B (2024) - providing 11 months of import cover.

Global Standing

India rose from 12th to 5th largest economy - poised to become 3rd largest by 2028.

Poverty Reduction

Poverty headcount fell from 37% to ~10% - approximately 260 million lifted out of poverty.
